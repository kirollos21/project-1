Domestic cat

There are more than 500 million domestic cats in the world have been associated with humans for nearly 10,000 years.

  • Lifespan indoor: 13 – 17 years.
  • Scientific name: Felis catus.
  • Mass: 3.6 – 4.5 kg (Adult).
  • Daily sleep: 12 – 16 hours.

The cat is a small carnivorous mammal, mostly with fur and claws. Cats average about 23–25 cm in height and 46 cm in head/body length (males being larger than females), with tails averaging 30 cm in length.
